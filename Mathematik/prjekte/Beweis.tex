\\\\

\\
Demnach: $\forall k \in \mathbb{N}(a_{x+k}=\varepsilon$)

$a_{x+1} = \varepsilon$\\

\]
\[  
  
  (x+2)! = (x+1)\cdot l\cdot 10^{k}+(x+1)\cdot 10^{k}\cdot \varepsilon\\
